Why Capybaras?

The world’s happiest animal deserves a future

Capybaras are famous for their calm, friendly nature and for getting along with almost every animal they meet. They are highly social, love relaxing in the water and have become internet icons for their peaceful personality.

That is why 100% of our profits are donated to conservation efforts that help protect Capybaras, wildlife and the places they depend on.
